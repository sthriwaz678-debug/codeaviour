The Challenge

Groundwater
Under Threat

Our groundwater faces critical threats — with no real-time monitoring in place, we're flying blind on one of our most vital resources.

📉
Depletion from Overuse
Excessive extraction rapidly draws down aquifer levels beyond natural recharge rates.
☠️
Pollution Contamination
Industrial discharge and agricultural runoff introduce harmful chemicals into groundwater.
🏙️
Urbanization Blocking Recharge
Concrete surfaces prevent rainwater from percolating, disrupting natural replenishment.
🚫
No Real-time Monitoring
Infrequent manual surveys leave communities blind to crises until it's too late to act.
